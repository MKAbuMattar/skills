# Content patterns (1-6)

These are the highest-impact patterns to remove. Content patterns inflate the importance of facts, attribute claims to nobody, and tack formulaic "Challenges" sections onto otherwise factual prose. Strip these first — the rest of the AI tells often disappear once the underlying scaffolding is gone.

---

## 1. Undue emphasis on significance, legacy, and broader trends

**Words to watch:** stands as, serves as, is a testament/reminder, a vital/significant/crucial/pivotal/key role/moment, underscores/highlights its importance, reflects broader, symbolizing its enduring/lasting, contributing to the, setting the stage for, marking/shaping the, represents/marks a shift, key turning point, evolving landscape, focal point, indelible mark, deeply rooted.

**Why it reads as AI:** LLMs puff up importance by adding statements about how arbitrary aspects represent or contribute to a broader topic. Real writers say what something is, not what it "stands as".

**Before:**
> The Statistical Institute of Catalonia was officially established in 1989, marking a pivotal moment in the evolution of regional statistics in Spain. This initiative was part of a broader movement across Spain to decentralize administrative functions and enhance regional governance.

**After:**
> The Statistical Institute of Catalonia was established in 1989 to collect and publish regional statistics independently from Spain's national statistics office.

**Fix recipe:** Delete the "marking/representing/symbolizing" clause entirely. Keep the fact, drop the meta-commentary.

---

## 2. Undue emphasis on notability and media coverage

**Words to watch:** independent coverage, local/regional/national media outlets, written by a leading expert, active social media presence, has been featured in.

**Why it reads as AI:** LLMs hit readers over the head with claims of notability, often listing sources without context. Humans either cite specifics (a real article, a real claim) or don't bring up notability at all.

**Before:**
> Her views have been cited in The New York Times, BBC, Financial Times, and The Hindu. She maintains an active social media presence with over 500,000 followers.

**After:**
> In a 2024 New York Times interview, she argued that AI regulation should focus on outcomes rather than methods.

**Fix recipe:** Replace the credentials list with a single concrete claim from one of those sources. If you don't have a concrete claim, cut the whole sentence.

---

## 3. Superficial analyses with -ing endings

**Words to watch:** highlighting, underscoring, emphasizing, ensuring, reflecting, symbolizing, contributing to, cultivating, fostering, encompassing, showcasing, demonstrating.

**Why it reads as AI:** AI chatbots tack present-participle phrases onto sentences to fake depth. The clause adds no information — it just gestures at importance.

**Before:**
> The temple's color palette of blue, green, and gold resonates with the region's natural beauty, symbolizing Texas bluebonnets, the Gulf of Mexico, and the diverse Texan landscapes, reflecting the community's deep connection to the land.

**After:**
> The temple uses blue, green, and gold colors. The architect said these were chosen to reference local bluebonnets and the Gulf coast.

**Fix recipe:** Cut everything from the participle to the period. If the meaning was load-bearing, rewrite as a separate sentence with a real subject and verb.

---

## 4. Promotional and advertisement-like language

**Words to watch:** boasts a, vibrant, rich (figurative), profound, enhancing its, showcasing, exemplifies, commitment to, natural beauty, nestled, in the heart of, groundbreaking (figurative), renowned, breathtaking, must-visit, stunning.

**Why it reads as AI:** LLMs default to tourism-brochure tone, especially for places, products, and "cultural heritage" topics. Real encyclopedic prose is neutral.

**Before:**
> Nestled within the breathtaking region of Gonder in Ethiopia, Alamata Raya Kobo stands as a vibrant town with a rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty.

**After:**
> Alamata Raya Kobo is a town in the Gonder region of Ethiopia, known for its weekly market and 18th-century church.

**Fix recipe:** Replace adjectives with concrete nouns. "Rich cultural heritage" → name an actual cultural feature. "Stunning natural beauty" → name the actual landform.

---

## 5. Vague attributions and weasel words

**Words to watch:** industry reports, observers have cited, experts argue, some critics argue, several sources/publications, it is widely believed, many have noted.

**Why it reads as AI:** AI chatbots attribute opinions to vague authorities without specific sources. Real journalism names the source; real opinion writing owns the opinion.

**Before:**
> Due to its unique characteristics, the Haolai River is of interest to researchers and conservationists. Experts believe it plays a crucial role in the regional ecosystem.

**After:**
> The Haolai River supports several endemic fish species, according to a 2019 survey by the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

**Fix recipe:** Either name the actual source (with date) or rewrite as a first-person opinion. "Experts believe X" → "According to [Source, Year]…" or just "X" if it's the writer's view.

---

## 6. Outline-like "challenges and future prospects" sections

**Words to watch:** Despite its [success], faces several challenges; Despite these challenges; Challenges and Legacy; Future Outlook; In conclusion.

**Why it reads as AI:** LLMs include formulaic "Challenges" sections that list generic problems followed by a generic upbeat closer. The shape is identical across topics.

**Before:**
> Despite its industrial prosperity, Korattur faces challenges typical of urban areas, including traffic congestion and water scarcity. Despite these challenges, with its strategic location and ongoing initiatives, Korattur continues to thrive as an integral part of Chennai's growth.

**After:**
> Traffic congestion increased after 2015 when three new IT parks opened. The municipal corporation began a stormwater drainage project in 2022 to address recurring floods.

**Fix recipe:** Delete the "Despite X, Y faces challenges… Despite these, Y continues to thrive" frame. Replace with concrete events, dates, and outcomes. No grand closer.
